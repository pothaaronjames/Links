How to Play

Each Round

  1. You are given a Start Actor and an End Actor.
  2. Your goal is to connect them through a chain of shared movies.
  3. Each step, enter a movie that the current actor appeared in.
  4. Then pick another actor from that movie to continue the chain.
  5. Reach the End Actor in as few steps as possible!

Each Game

A game consists of 5 rounds. Your final game score is the sum of points from all 5 rounds. Try to maximize your total!

Scoring Per Round

1–2 steps Genius
3–4 steps Great
5 steps Good
